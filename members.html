Featured Member: Lauren Ehehalt

Lauren is a junior from Michigan in the chemistry department with a certificate in planets and life. When she's not in a chemistry article reading frenzy or desperately working on core lab psets in the co-op until obscenely late hours, she can be found playing taiko with Tora Taiko, working as Student House Manager at Richardson Auditorium, or setting stuff on fire (safely!) with the chemical society. Her life goals include helping to create a medicine to fix her mosquito allergy and to curate the perfect 80s rock playlist.
